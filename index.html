Why Tiny Melody?

Tiny Melody is a beautifully simple music box app for iPhone. Tap the golden keys, record a tune, and share it as a tiny musical gift — no music theory, no login, no ads. Just you and a little music box.

Key Features

🎹 Play Instantly

15 golden keys with distinct colors and warm, real-time music box sound. Multi-touch and slide-playing supported.

⏺ Record & Save

Tap record, play your melody, and save it with a name. Tiny Melody captures the notes — not the noise around you.

✨ Smooth Timing

One tap gently corrects your rhythm while keeping your melody yours. Original and Smooth versions are both preserved.

🎨 Three Sound Presets

Classic (bright), Wooden (warm), Dream (airy). Each preset transforms the character of your music box.

📦 Built-in Classics

Six beloved melodies included — Twinkle Twinkle, Ode to Joy, Brahms' Lullaby, and more. Make them yours with one tap.

How It Works

  1. Open the app — the keys are waiting.
  2. Tap to play — each key glows and sings.
  3. Hit Record — a 3-2-1 countdown prepares you.
  4. Play your melody — 15 notes, endless possibilities.
  5. Save & name it — it lives in your collection.
